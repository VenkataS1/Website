Entrepenuerial Society

Sharing Excitement. Sharing Ideas. Sharing Community.
RES is the primary hub of student innovation at Rutgers University - New Brunswick. Our membership spans all academic disciplines from Finance to Computer Science to English and everything in between. Our network is composed of active student organizations and dedicated faculty across Rutgers' undergraduate schools -- all dedicated to providing support and resources to anyone who wants to build an independent project or business here at Rutgers.

RES acts as a hub for sharing ideas and enjoying activities based on our common interest. Rutgers Entrepreneurial Society promotes and expects a non-judgmental and supportive attitude from participants. If you’re interested in joining or would like to learn more, please don’t hesitate to get in touch.
